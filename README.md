## Who I Am

I'm an **AI Product Manager** with roots in enterprise integration engineering. I came up building the systems before I owned the products — which means I read architecture diagrams the way most PMs read PRDs, and I can challenge an AI infrastructure decision at the same depth I can challenge a user story.

At **Avasant**, I led the product build of an enterprise S2P + CLM platform — contracts, pricing intelligence, SLA governance, and AI extraction — integrating across SAP, Salesforce, and ServiceNow. Along the way I went from understanding how AI could automate contract metadata to defining what "AI-ready" means at an enterprise data model level.

What makes me different: I don't just add AI to roadmaps. I **think in AI-first architectures** — where the model sits, what it needs to be reliable at scale, how it changes the user's mental model, and what happens when it gets it wrong.

**Looking for:** Senior AI PM · Staff Technical PM · AI Platform · Enterprise SaaS · Developer Tools · Bangalore / Remote

## How I Use AI — Every Day

AI isn't a tool I pick up occasionally. It's embedded in how I work, how I build, and how I think about products.

**Professionally:**
- Restructured product discovery workflows using AI-assisted synthesis — compressing the gap between raw signal and spec-ready insight
- Built and use [`pm-tools-ai-workflows`](https://github.com/rahulchaki07/pm-tools-ai-workflows) — a personal CLI toolkit for PRD drafting, story scoring, competitive teardowns, and interview synthesis
- Driving AI feature definition at Avasant — extraction pipelines, document classification, LLM integration specs — from business case through to acceptance criteria
- Embedded AI as a co-pilot across the product development lifecycle: problem framing → API schema review → edge case discovery → QA heuristics

**Personally:**
- Building and shipping full-stack AI products end-to-end — RAG pipelines, GPT-4o and Claude integrations, multi-modal inputs — not as a hobby, as a method of validating product decisions before writing a spec
- Open-sourcing AI PM tools and frameworks for the community, because good AI product thinking should compound beyond one team
- Using AI to drive measurable outcomes: reduced time-to-insight, improved decision quality, and shipped products with clearer user value — not just faster output

### Open Source — For the PM & Builder Community

> Tools I built to solve real workflow problems. Free, inspectable, and built the way a PM thinks — problem first. More products and small utilities will be open-sourced as they mature.

| Project | Problem It Solves | What It Does |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|

### Products I'm Currently Building

> Each starts with a real user problem, an AI-first architecture, and a thesis about where the market is underserving people.

| Product | Who It's For | The Problem | The AI Angle |
|---------|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| **The Smart PM** | Product Managers | Roadmaps, PRDs, OKRs, and user feedback all live in different tools with no intelligence layer connecting them | AI-assisted PRD generation, sprint story scoring, feedback sentiment, stakeholder mapping |
| **LeapFrogX** | Job seekers | The job search is still a spreadsheet problem — no coaching layer exists between "apply" and "offer" | Per-role AI prep journeys, mock interviews with real-time scoring, ATS resume intelligence, 4-week career roadmap |
| **DocuMind** | Knowledge workers & teams | Document intelligence is locked inside expensive SaaS — you can't own your data or your queries | Self-hosted RAG: semantic search across multiple docs, streaming cited answers, no vendor lock-in |
| **ClosetIQ** | Indian households | No wardrobe app understands the complexity of ethnic wear sets or gives weather-aware outfit intelligence | AI item scanning, Set linking (saree + blouse + petticoat as one unit), 3-mode outfit suggestions tuned to context |
| **EasyNews** | Working professionals | News is generic and passive — it doesn't adapt to your role, domain knowledge, or how well you retain what you read | Persona-aware feed, AI simplification by reading level, concept explainer on demand, daily quiz for active retention |
| **Clarity Finance** | Indian home & car buyers | No tool gives a clear, honest verdict on whether a major purchase fits your actual financial reality | 4-dimensional affordability engine: EMI ratio · liquidity impact · monthly surplus · emergency fund — clear YES / NO / WAIT |
| **SimplySocial** | Creators & small teams | Managing multiple social platforms means five logins, five UIs, and no unified view of what's working | Unified dashboard: compose, schedule, and cross-platform analytics without switching context |
| **Simply Wealth** | Families | Family finances are managed in spreadsheets with no joint visibility, no goal tracking, and no shared picture of net worth | Family-scoped finance app: transactions, investments, budgets, goals, EMI planning, multi-member profiles |
| **ContractAI** | Legal & procurement teams | Contract review is slow, manual, and inconsistent — the same risks get missed at different levels of scrutiny | AI extraction of risks, opportunities, and legal findings — sorted by severity, reviewable in a structured dashboard |
